# Judge Demo Flow (3–5 minutes)

1. **Dashboard** — portfolio KPIs, band/readiness/trend/sector charts.
2. **Attention signals** — point to sudden-deterioration + low-confidence entries.
3. Click a **deteriorating MSME** → Financial Health Card.
4. Walk the **score components** and **six dimensions** (evidence + risk signals).
5. Open **Temporal Twin** — real monthly re-scoring; highlight change points.
6. Show an **Unusual Financial Pattern** (review signal, never "fraud").
7. Open **Explainability** — honest rules fallback; SHAP unavailable is stated, not faked.
8. **Improve My Score** — reduce receivable days / raise reserve → real engine re-score.
9. Note **Data Confidence** (separate axis) and **Credit Readiness** (never "Loan Approved").
10. Finish on **Transparency** — formula, weights, bands, provenance, limitations.
